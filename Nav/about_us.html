OUR STORY

FluOrganik are a group of friends united around a common passion for psychedelic and alternative art in all its forms.

In music and visuals, we have an attraction for all kind of organic textures, natural flows, molecular forms.. Hence the name "FluOrganik" as an Organic Flow.


We started doing liquid light shows in 2016 with the "FluOrganik Light Show" project. Then came the association "FluOrganik Crew" born in early 2019.

We started by setting up chill-out spaces in different events, and the idea of incorporate psychedelic visuals immediately became obvious. From there, we started to learn about liquid projections with old overhead projectors.


We perform mainly in the world of electronic music, we have played with prestigious chill-out music artists on the stages of several festivals (Hadra Trance Festival, Son Libre Festival).

The collective has since diversified, in particular with the acquisition of our sound system, which enables us to organise our own events.

Today, as well as performing, we organise some musical events (such as the Tropi'Kermesse in 2022 and 2023).

OUR TEAM

Originally founded by 4 passionate friends, the FluOrganik Crew now has around ten members.

Each member brings his or her own speciality to the collective, enabling us to realise new projects and think bigger and bigger !

From budding sound engineers to video enthusiasts and tinkerers of all kinds, this diverse team is ready to take on any challenge !
